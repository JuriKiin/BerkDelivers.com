Berk Delivers' founder, Samuel Webb, is a young entrepreneur with a passion for the Berkshires. Over the past few years, he has strived to stimulate the local economy through on demand transportation and delivery. During COVID-19 he saw many restaurants and small businesses experience a significant decrease in sales within the first few weeks of social distancing. He started Berk Delivers to help local restaurants increase their sales by offering on demand delivery to their customers. Learn More
